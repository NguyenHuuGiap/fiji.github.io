The "Files not up-to-date" dialog is perhaps slightly misleading. What it is saying is "these are the files whose integrity I can not verify." The explanation for why they can't be verified is in the parentheses, and is important to understand.

The majority of "not up-to-date" files in your report are "LOCAL_ONLY", which means that they exist on your local machine but not on any of the update sites you have enabled (ImageJ and Fiji in this case). And because they are unknown, there would be no way for them to be updated.

LOCAL_ONLY files would only be a problem if they were interfering somehow with the classes that are distributed with Fiji/ImageJ... you don't actually have any "outdated" files in need of updating. As you do not have a lot of local changes, I think it is worth trying to reproduce the TIFF failures on our end.

Let me know when you upload your sample data, and I'll take a look. Thanks!
- Mark
