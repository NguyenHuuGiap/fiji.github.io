Comment 3 Wayne Rasband 2016-03-15 17:03:05 CDT 
What you can do is download ij.jar from

    https://imagej.nih.gov/ij/upgrade/ij.jar

copy it to the Fiji.app/jars folder, delete ij-1.50g.jar from the same folder and restart Fiji.
