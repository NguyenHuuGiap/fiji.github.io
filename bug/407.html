Comment 1 Wayne Rasband 2012-02-24 17:31:40 CST 
The "Multiple Image Processor" runs on Macs and Linux if you change

   if(IJ.isWindows()){
      IJ.runMacroFile(FileMacro,"");
   }else{      
      IJ.run("Run... ", "run=" + FileMacro);
   }

to

   IJ.runMacroFile(FileMacro,"");

in MultipleImageProcessor.java.
