Comment 1 Curtis Rueden 2010-06-22 00:09:04 CDT 
This was a problem with HandleExtraFileTypes, due to Albert's change:

http://pacific.mpi-cbg.de/cgi-bin/gitweb.cgi?p=fiji.git;a=blobdiff;f=src-plugins/IO_/HandleExtraFileTypes.java;h=510819dd545da045a99559276e034b4fd2e830af;hp=d783ed549511a19b9597d4360cf8741af70f8318;hb=a7a9545ad6573e3d8db25b6af844d0d6ad253c05;hpb=f3c25e1c242cc7c170135cfac816b07898c63fba

The tryPlugIn method was tripping the IMAGE_OPENED flag even when the I/O plugin failed to run. The fix was very simple:

http://pacific.mpi-cbg.de/cgi-bin/gitweb.cgi?p=fiji.git;a=blobdiff;f=src-plugins/IO_/HandleExtraFileTypes.java;h=51007d608bfb4f1bf30e5633933cbda8cc4b5b9e;hp=9f265e21c8d7c45ddc72b60e4b99a384f3618770;hb=4ad9868564476ee704cd1ed1fc844b10ba61ff7d;hpb=3680f84c2360e5f64a85c9cb7d57db02360138f1

I am not 100% certain this fix is correct, but it does fix the drag-and-drop issue with ZVI files.
